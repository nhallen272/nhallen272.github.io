About Me

My name is Nathan Allen. I am Old Dominion University graduate with a bachelor of science in cybersecurity and a minor in Computer Science. I have 3-4 years of programming and web development experience and have an interest in embedded systems/IoT development such as the Raspberry Pi and ESP32. Since I started at Tidewater Community College in 2019, with the goal of starting a career in the computing industry, I have actively sought to improve my programming experience and skill both in and outside of the classroom. In school I learned C++, but meanwhile, I was also learning Python, embedded systems and IoT platforms such as ESP32, Thingsboard, and Arduino.
